## What Is a Flipkart Affiliate Link?

A **Flipkart affiliate link** is a trackable URL that includes affiliate attribution parameters (such as a click ID / sub ID). When a user clicks your link and completes an eligible purchase, the affiliate network can attribute the order to your account.

### Normal link vs affiliate link (important)
- **Normal Flipkart product URL:** may not be attributed to you
- **Affiliate link:** includes tracking, so your clicks and conversions can be recorded

## How Click Tracking and Conversion Tracking Works

### Click Tracking (immediate)
When someone clicks your Earnko link:
1. A click is recorded (unique click ID)
2. The user is redirected through the tracking flow
3. Flipkart opens
4. Your click count updates in the dashboard

### Conversion Tracking (usually delayed)
Conversions are not always instant because:
- orders can be cancelled or returned
- the merchant confirms the sale later
- affiliate networks send reports periodically

This is normal—affiliate marketing reporting often has a delay.

## Common Problems (and Fixes)

### Problem: “Clicks are coming but conversions are zero”
Possible reasons:
- user didn’t complete purchase
- user cancelled/returned
- tracking window expired
- user used a competing coupon/affiliate source
- user switched device/app flow and attribution changed

### Problem: “Link opens a different page”
Fix:
- ensure you pasted the correct product page URL
- try generating again using the clean product page URL
- avoid copying links that contain extra redirect layers
